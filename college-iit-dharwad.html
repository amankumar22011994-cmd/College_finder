Key Highlights

Permanent Campus

Recently shifted to its own 470-acre permanent campus at Chikkamalligawad, featuring world-class architecture and labs.

Global Mentorship

Mentored by IIT Bombay during its early years, inheriting high academic standards and a strong research culture.

Strategic Location

Situated in the Dharwad-Hubballi industrial belt, offering great opportunities for industry-academia collaboration.

Research Centers

Focuses on advanced research in AI, IoT, and Sustainable Energy through specialized centers of excellence.
