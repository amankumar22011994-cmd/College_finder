Why NIT Kurukshetra?

Siemens CoE

Houses a multi-million dollar Siemens Center of Excellence focusing on digital design, manufacturing, and automation technologies.

Historical Legacy

Originally Regional Engineering College (REC), it is one of India's oldest technical institutions with a vast global alumni network.

AI & Robotics Hub

Introduced specialized B.Tech programs in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Robotics to match industry 4.0 standards.

Research Power

Equipped with a Supercomputer "Param Shavak" for advanced computational research in data science and engineering.

Campus Facilities & Culture
🏠

Centralized Hostels

Excellent housing for all students, with specialized Mega-Hostels providing high-speed internet and modern mess facilities.

🎭

Confluence

The annual cultural fest, a vibrant three-day carnival attracting talent from across North India for music, dance, and fashion.

⚙️

Techspardha

A national-level techno-management festival featuring hackathons, drone races, and deep-tech workshops.

📚

Central Library

Housed in a 3,600 sq. m. building, it offers 24/7 access to over 175,000 books and 13,000+ digital journals.

🏟️

Sports Complex

Includes an Olympic-size swimming pool, floodlit basketball courts, and a cricket stadium with indoor sports halls.

🧘

Campus Serenity

Located on the banks of the historic Brahma Sarovar, the campus blends ancient wisdom with modern tech education.

An alumnus of the 1992 batch who rose through the ranks to lead Wipro, one of India's biggest IT services giants, globally.

Sudarshan Sarma

VP, Capgemini (Former Google Exec)

A senior leader who has held top executive positions at Google and Uber, contributing significantly to the digital growth in India.

Rakesh Bakshi

Recognized for his immense contribution to the renewable energy sector in India, specifically in the field of wind energy.
