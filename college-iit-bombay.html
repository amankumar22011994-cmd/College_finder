About IIT Bombay

Legacy & Prestige

Founded in 1958 with Soviet assistance. Consistently ranked among the top 3 IITs and top 200 universities globally.

Research & Innovation

20+ research centers including CRNTS, SAIF, and SysCon. Files 150+ patents annually with 1,000+ active research projects.

Entrepreneurship

SINE incubator has nurtured 100+ startups. Notable alumni ventures include Naukri.com, Ola, and Practo.

Global Collaborations

Active MoUs with MIT, Stanford, TU Munich, and 100+ global universities. Students from 40+ countries collaborate here.
