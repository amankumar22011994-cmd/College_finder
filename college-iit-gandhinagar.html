About IIT Gandhinagar

Student-Centric Vision

IITGN is known for breaking the traditional 'mould'. It offers a flexible curriculum where students can easily take minors in humanities, arts, or management alongside their core engineering degrees.

Global Exposure

A staggering 40% of undergraduate students travel abroad for internships or semesters. Partnerships with Caltech, Cambridge, and JAIST Japan provide unparalleled international research opportunities.

Invention & Design

Host to the 'Invention Factory India'—a unique 6-week summer program where students from across India create and patent new physical products from scratch with high-end mentorship.

Eco-Sustainability

The first campus in India to be awarded the 5-Star GRIHA LD rating. It features a zero-discharge water system, solar harvesting, and non-monolithic architectural aesthetics.
