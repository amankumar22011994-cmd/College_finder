About NIT Delhi

Rapid Growth

Started in 2010, NIT Delhi has quickly climbed the rankings to become a preferred choice due to its strategic location in the National Capital Region (NCR).

Capital Advantage

Proximity to India's top tech hubs and government ministries provides students with unparalleled internship and industry networking opportunities.

Modern Infrastructure

The transition to the new permanent campus in Narela has introduced world-class smart classrooms, high-tech labs, and centralized air-conditioning.

Research Culture

Strong focus on Sustainable Energy, VLSI Design, and Artificial Intelligence, with numerous projects funded by DST and MeitY.
