Why NIT Raipur?

Historical Foundation

Inaugurated by Pt. Jawaharlal Nehru, it is one of the oldest and most respected technical institutions in central India.

Core Engineering Hub

Located in the heart of India's industrial belt, offering unparalleled exposure to Mining, Metallurgy, and Steel sectors.

Centralized Location

Situated on G.E. Road in the state capital, providing excellent connectivity and urban amenities to students.

Diversified Placement

Strong performance across both Software/IT and Core sectors, with significant improvements in the 2025 cycle.
