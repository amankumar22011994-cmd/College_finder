About IIIT Kancheepuram

Legacy & Prestige

Established in 2007 as IIITDM Kancheepuram (Chennai) — one of India's unique IIIT-DM institutions blending CS, ECE, and mechanical engineering with product design. Located near Chennai in Tamil Nadu.

Design Focus

Unique blend of IT and product design education. Curriculum covers CAD, CAM, 3D printing, IoT, and embedded systems — preparing students for hardware product development roles.

Research & Innovation

Active research in IoT, medical devices, robotics, and smart systems. Collaborates with Chennai's auto and IT industry for applied research projects.

South India Advantage

Located near Chennai — Tamil Nadu's IT and manufacturing hub. Access to Hyundai, Ford, Nokia, and numerous tech MNCs nearby for internships and placements.
