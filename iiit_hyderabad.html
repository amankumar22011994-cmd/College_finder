About IIIT Hyderabad

Legacy & Prestige

Founded in 1998 as India's first IIIT. A deemed university with a strong research culture — often called the "IIT of IIITs" for CS and AI. Consistently in top 50 NIRF.

Research & Innovation

Home to 20+ research centers including LTRC (language tech), CVIT (computer vision), and RRC (robotics). 300+ research publications annually and strong PhD program.

Dual Degree Advantage

Unique 5-year Dual Degree (B.Tech + MS by Research) program. Students graduate with both a B.Tech and a research master's — highly valued by top global companies and universities.

Global Collaborations

Active research tie-ups with CMU, MIT, Microsoft Research, Google Research, and IBM. Students regularly intern at top global labs and research centers worldwide.

Campus Life
🏠

Hostels

Separate hostels for boys and girls. All students guaranteed on-campus accommodation in modern facilities.

🎭

Felicity

Annual cultural and tech fest with music, coding competitions, robotics events and management games drawing 10,000+ participants.

🔬

Research Labs

CVIT, LTRC, RRC, SPCRC — 20+ world-class research labs where UG students actively contribute to published research.

🏸

Sports Facilities

Badminton courts, basketball ground, cricket pitch, volleyball, football field, and a well-equipped gym on campus.

🌆

Gachibowli Location

Located in Hyderabad's IT hub — surrounded by Google, Microsoft, Amazon & top MNC offices. Internship opportunities at doorstep.

🍽️

Dining

Multiple mess and canteen options. South Indian, North Indian and continental cuisine available. Separate mess for boys and girls.

📚

Central Library

Well-stocked library with digital access to ACM, IEEE, Springer journals. Quiet reading zones and 24-hour study rooms available.

🚀

Student Clubs

50+ clubs — Ping (coding), Robocon, Photography, Music, Drama, Quiz, and the famous IIIT-H Open Source community (OSDG).
