About IIIT Allahabad

Legacy & Prestige

Founded in 1999 — one of the first IIITs in India, established by the Union Government as an Institute of National Importance. Strong legacy in IT and CS with alumni across the globe.

Research & Innovation

Active research in HCI, computer vision, NLP, and cybersecurity. Multiple DST and SERB funded projects. 300+ publications annually with close to 15 research labs on campus.

Industry Connect

Strong placement record with IT majors like TCS, Infosys, and Wipro alongside product companies. Located in Prayagraj with easy access to Lucknow and Varanasi corridors.

Global Reach

Research collaborations with international universities and active support for MS/PhD applications. Regular seminars and workshops with global tech professionals.
