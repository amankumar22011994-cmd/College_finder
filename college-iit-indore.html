About IIT Indore

Modern Infrastructure

Located in Simrol, the campus is fully residential with state-of-the-art labs and hostels. It is one of the most aesthetically planned new-generation IITs.

Research Ranking

IIT Indore consistently ranks very high in global research citations (THE World University Rankings), often beating older IITs in research impact per faculty.

Space & Astronomy

It is the only IIT to have a dedicated department for Astronomy, Astrophysics, and Space Engineering, working closely with ISRO and global space agencies.

Industry Connect

Strong ties with the industrial hub of Pithampur and Indore, facilitating real-world projects, internships, and high-quality placements in core sectors.
