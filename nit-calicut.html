About NIT Calicut

Legacy & Vision

Formerly Calicut Regional Engineering College (CREC), it is one of the oldest NITs. Known for its rigorous academic culture and top-tier research in the South Indian circuit.

Supercomputing Power

Home to 'PICO', a high-performance supercomputing facility, enabling research in data science, climate modeling, and bioinformatics.

Startup Culture

One of the most active Technology Business Incubators (TBI) among NITs, having successfully launched several national-level tech startups.

Ecological Balance

Located at the foothills of the Western Ghats, the campus is a biodiversity hub and follows a strict plastic-free and green-energy mandate.

Campus Life
🏠

Hostels

12 hostels for men and 2 for women. Known for a mix of historic and modern infrastructure with high-speed campus LAN.

🎭

Ragam

One of the largest cultural fests in South India. Features high-profile pro-shows and national level dance and music competitions.

⚙️

Tathva

The annual techno-management fest. Host to robotics, hackathons, and innovative project exhibitions from across the country.

🏟️

Sports Culture

Dedicated physical education department with facilities for football, basketball, and a newly built indoor sports arena.

🌳

Western Ghats

Located in a lush green valley, the campus offers a serene environment with unique flora and fauna typical of Kerala.

🍽️

Mess & Food

Managed by student committees, offering diverse menus from Kerala Sadhya to North Indian delicacies and night canteens.

📚

Digital Library

Central library with access to IEEE, Springer, and other high-impact journals, plus a 24-hour reading room facility.

💻

CCC

The Central Computing Center provides robust computing resources and server hosting for student-led tech projects.
