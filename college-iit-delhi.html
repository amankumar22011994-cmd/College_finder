About IIT Delhi

Legacy & Prestige

Founded in 1961 as the College of Engineering & Technology, upgraded to IIT in 1963. Consistently ranked #2 in India (NIRF) and top 200 globally — the capital's premier technical institution.

Research & Innovation

250+ sponsored research projects and 25+ centres of excellence including IITD-AIA, IRCAI, and Yardi School of AI. Files 200+ patents annually with ₹600 Cr+ research funding.

Entrepreneurship

FITT (Foundation for Innovation & Technology Transfer) has incubated 200+ startups. Notable ventures include IndiaMart, Housing.com, and Zetwerk. Hosts IITD's annual E-Summit.

Global Collaborations

Active MoUs with MIT, CMU, ETH Zurich, NUS, and 120+ global institutions. Hosts International students from 40+ countries and runs dual-degree programs with top global universities.

Campus Life
🏙️

Prime Delhi Location

325-acre campus in Hauz Khas, South Delhi — minutes from metro, malls, and the heart of the capital. Best located IIT in India.

🎭

Rendezvous

One of India's largest college cultural fests with 80,000+ footfall, celebrity performances, competitions in music, drama, and fine arts.

⚙️

Tryst

IIT Delhi's premier science & tech festival featuring AI hackathons, robotics, drone racing, and talks by global innovators.

🏠

Hostels

13 hostels (11 boys, 2 girls) with guaranteed UG accommodation. Each hostel has its own identity, mess, and vibrant hostel culture.

🏊

Sports Complex

Olympic-size pool, cricket ground, football & hockey fields, squash courts, badminton halls, and a modern gym facility.

🍽️

Dining

Multiple mess halls, Amul outlet, Nescafé kiosks, and the iconic Nilgiri's canteen. North Indian, South Indian & fast food options.

📚

Central Library

5 lakh+ books, journals, and e-resources. One of the best-stocked engineering libraries in India with extended exam-season hours.

🚀

Student Clubs

80+ clubs — IITD Racing (Formula Student), Team Anveshak, coding society, astronomy, quizzing, music, dance, and debate clubs.
