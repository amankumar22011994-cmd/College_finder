Why NIT Uttarakhand?

Rapid Growth

Transitioning to a massive permanent campus in Sumari with modern infrastructure and state-of-the-art labs.

Himalayan Location

Located in the heart of the Himalayas, offering a peaceful and pollution-free environment conducive to deep focus.

Student Innovation

Strong focus on grassroots technical projects and research, with high faculty-to-student interaction due to smaller batch sizes.

Diverse Tech Culture

Home to active coding clubs and robotics teams that participate in national level hackathons and competitions.
