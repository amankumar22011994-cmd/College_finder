About IIIT Bangalore

Legacy & Prestige

Founded in 1999 in the heart of India's Silicon Valley — Electronic City, Bengaluru. Known for its industry-integrated curriculum and strong corporate connections in the tech ecosystem.

Research & Innovation

Active research in data engineering, digital society, and networked intelligence. IIIT-B has published 500+ research papers and collaborates closely with industry partners on applied research.

Industry Integration

Unique iMTech (integrated M.Tech) program lets students work part-time in industry while studying. Strong partnerships with Infosys, Wipro, Bosch, and SAP for live projects and internships.

Global Collaborations

Tie-ups with Georgia Tech, University of Illinois, TU Delft, and NUS Singapore. Students benefit from joint research, exchange programs, and co-supervised PhD opportunities.

🏠

Hostels

On-campus hostel facilities for both boys and girls. Modern amenities with Wi-Fi, laundry, and 24-hour security.

🎭

Verve

Annual cultural fest featuring music, dance, drama competitions and celebrity performances drawing students from across Bengaluru.

⚙️

Ingenium

Annual tech fest with hackathons, coding contests, robotics challenges and talks by industry leaders from the Bengaluru tech ecosystem.

Electronic City Location

Campus is located in Electronic City — India's largest IT hub. Infosys, Wipro, HCL campuses are walking distance away — ideal for networking.

🍽️

Dining

Cafeteria with South Indian and North Indian menus. Multiple food stalls on campus. Proximity to Electronic City eateries and restaurants.
