About NIT Durgapur

Rich History

Established as Regional Engineering College (REC) Durgapur in 1960, it is one of the oldest and most respected technical institutes in India.

Steel City Advantage

Located in the heart of Durgapur (India's Steel City), it offers deep industrial exposure through partnerships with SAIL, DSP, and other manufacturing giants.

Expansive Campus

The lush 187-acre campus is a self-contained ecosystem with high-tech research labs, vast playgrounds, and residential colonies.

Innovation Focus

Houses a Microchip Development Center and maintains active MOUs with top-tier institutes like IIT Roorkee and international universities.

Campus Life & Traditions
🏠

Hall of Residences

Contains 9 boys' and 4 girls' hostels with a rich "Hall Life" culture and competitive inter-hall events.

🎭

Recstacy

The annual cultural fest, one of the biggest in Eastern India, drawing massive crowds for star nights and fashion shows.

⚙️

Aavishkar / Aarohan

Flagship technical and techno-management fests featuring drone racing, coding marathons, and industry guest lectures.

🏟️

Lords Ground

The legendary main sports ground hosting inter-NIT meets, local tournaments, and daily practice sessions.

👨‍💻

RECursion

A thriving coding club that drives the competitive programming culture, resulting in strong Google Summer of Code (GSoC) results.

🚉

Connectivity

Located just 8.5 km from Durgapur Railway Station and 14 km from Kazi Nazrul Islam (Andal) Airport.
