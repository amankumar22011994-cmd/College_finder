About IIT Roorkee

Legacy & Prestige

Founded in 1847 as Thomason College of Civil Engineering, IIT Roorkee is Asia's oldest technical institution. It became an IIT in 2001 and has a 175+ year legacy of engineering excellence.

Research & Innovation

Home to 350+ labs including CBRI, WRDM, and earthquake engineering centres. Annual sponsored research funding exceeds ₹350 crore. Files 200+ patents annually.

Entrepreneurship

TIDES (Technology Incubation & Enterprise Development Society) has nurtured 200+ startups. IIT Roorkee's E-Cell is one of the most active in northern India.

Global Collaborations

Active MoUs with TU Delft, University of Stuttgart, University of Waterloo, NUS Singapore, and 80+ global universities. Strong exchange programs in civil and earthquake engineering.

Campus Life
🏛️

Heritage Architecture

The iconic Main Building (1847) and Thompson Block are Grade-I heritage structures. The campus blends colonial-era Gothic architecture with modern academic facilities.

🎭

Thomso

IIT Roorkee's annual cultural fest (named after founder James Thomason) features celebrity performances, competitions in music, dance, drama, and art with 40,000+ footfall.

⚙️

Cognizance

Northern India's premier technical fest. Hosts robotics, hackathons, paper presentations, lectures by tech leaders, and entrepreneurship challenges with 30,000+ participants.

🏊

Sports Facilities

Olympic-size swimming pool, cricket ground, football field, basketball and volleyball courts, squash courts, a modern gym, and dedicated tracks for athletics.

🏠

Hostels (Bhavans)

12 residential bhavans including dedicated hostels for women. All UG students are guaranteed accommodation. Each bhavan has its own cultural events and mess facilities.

🍽️

Dining

Multiple bhavan messes, Nesci Hub, SAC food court, and canteens offering North Indian, South Indian, and fast food. The Roorkee bazaar nearby is also popular among students.

📚

James Thomason Library

One of India's oldest technical libraries with 3.5 lakh+ books, rare manuscripts from 1847, e-journals, and modern reading areas. Named after the institute's founder.

🚀

Student Clubs

120+ clubs — Team Robocon, Roorkee Open Source Society, astronomy, quizzing, photography, SAE India student chapter, music, dance, and many technical societies.
