About NIT Agartala

North-East Excellence

Founded in 1965 as Tripura Engineering College. It is the premier technical institution in the North-Eastern region of India, offering a blend of traditional and modern engineering.

PARAM Ishan

Houses the PARAM Ishan supercomputer, the fastest in the North-East, facilitating advanced research in computational fluid dynamics and weather forecasting.

Holistic Development

Strong emphasis on both academics and extracurriculars. The campus is fully residential with state-of-the-art labs and a massive central library.

Technological Hub

Strategic location near Agartala city allows for strong local industry interaction and government consultancy projects within the state of Tripura.

Campus Life
🏠

Hostels

10 hostels for boys and girls. Modern infrastructure with spacious rooms and scenic views of the surrounding green hills.

🎭

Moksha

The annual cultural fest of NIT Agartala. It's the largest in the state, featuring ethnic nights, concerts, and literary events.

⚙️

Aayam

The national-level technical symposium. Features robotics competitions, coding challenges, and innovation expos attracting talent from across India.

🏟️

Sports Arena

A massive indoor stadium and outdoor grounds for cricket, football, and athletics. Host to several inter-NIT sports meets.

🌳

Lush Green Campus

366 acres of naturally beautiful landscape, offering a peaceful and focused environment for academic growth.

🍽️

Dining & Cafes

Diverse mess menus covering North, South, and local North-Eastern cuisines. Multiple night canteens for late-night study sessions.

📚

Central Library

Digitalized library with vast electronic resources and a calm reading environment. Open late for exam preparation.

💻

Computing Facility

24/7 access to high-speed internet and specialized computer labs equipped with industry-standard software.
