About NIT Hamirpur

Scenic Hill Station

Known as one of the most beautiful campuses in India, set amidst pine forests with a breathtaking view of the Dhauladhar ranges.

Academic Rigor

Offers dual-degree programs and integrated M.Tech courses that are highly sought after by global tech giants like Google and Nvidia.

Research Excellence

Home to the 'C-DAC' center for excellence in high-end computing and advanced material research laboratories.
