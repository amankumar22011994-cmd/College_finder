About MNIT Jaipur

Heart of Pink City

Strategically located in the center of Jaipur, the campus offers unparalleled urban connectivity combined with a serene, lush green environment.

Legacy of Excellence

As one of the oldest Regional Engineering Colleges (MREC), it holds a rich tradition of producing top-tier bureaucrats, researchers, and entrepreneurs.

MIIC Incubation

The MNIT Innovation and Incubation Centre (MIIC) is one of Rajasthan's premier hubs for student-led startups and patent filings.

Global Recognition

Ranked consistently among the top 50 engineering institutes in India, with strong international collaborations with universities in USA and Germany.

Campus Facilities & Culture
🏠

Centralized Hostels

Excellent housing for all students, with separate girls' and boys' blocks featuring modern amenities and lush green surroundings.

🎭

Blitzschlag

The annual cultural fest, one of the largest in North India, featuring Battle of Bands, Panache (Fashion), and Ramba Samba (Dance).

⚙️

Sphinx

The flagship technical festival known for high-stakes hackathons, robotics challenges, and guest lectures by global innovators.

📚

Central Library

A massive, air-conditioned facility with over 150,000 books and access to top global research journals and digital resources.

🏟️

Sports Arena

State-of-the-art sports complex with floodlit courts for tennis, basketball, and a sprawling cricket/football ground.

📍

Location Perk

Located on JLN Marg, providing immediate access to world-class malls, heritage sites, and major transport hubs.
