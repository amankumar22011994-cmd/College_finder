Why MANIT Bhopal?

Central Location

Located in the 'City of Lakes', Bhopal, providing excellent connectivity and a pleasant climate for student life.

Legacy of REC

Originally established as one of the first Regional Engineering Colleges, it holds a deep-rooted academic legacy in India.

Green Infrastructure

A sprawling 650-acre verdant plateau campus, offering dedicated zones for sports, innovation, and research labs.

Modern Curriculum

Home to cutting-edge dual degree programs like Mathematics & Data Science and EV Engineering to match industry 4.0 needs.

Distinguished Alumni Network (Wall of Fame)

Ajit Jogi

First Chief Minister of Chhattisgarh

A Mechanical Engineering gold medalist from the 1968 batch. He transitioned from a brilliant engineer to an IAS officer and eventually the first CM of Chhattisgarh.

Suresh Pachauri

Former Union Minister of India

An alumnus who served as the Minister of State in the Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions and Parliamentary Affairs.

Naveen Jha

CEO, Deshpande Foundation

A prominent social entrepreneur leading massive scaling of developmental programs and fostering startup ecosystems across rural India.

Rambabu Gupta

Chairman, GMD Group

A leading industrialist who has made significant contributions to the manufacturing and infrastructure sectors in India and abroad.
