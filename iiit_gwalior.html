About IIIT Gwalior

Legacy & Prestige

India's very first IIIT, established in 1997 — predating all others. Named after former PM Atal Bihari Vajpayee. Located in Gwalior, MP with a unique blend of IT and Management education.

Research & Innovation

Active research in data mining, bioinformatics, network security, and operations research. Strong legacy of student research projects and publications in national and international journals.

IT + Management

Unique IPG (Integrated Post Graduate) program combining B.Tech + MBA in 5 years — the only such program in any IIIT. Excellent for students wanting both technical and managerial careers.

Industry Connect

Regular industry interactions with Gwalior-based firms and Madhya Pradesh government IT projects. Collaboration with NIC and STPI for research and internship opportunities.
