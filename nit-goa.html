About NIT Goa

New Permanent Campus

Originally operating from Farmagudi, NIT Goa moved to its permanent 114-acre campus at Cuncolim, South Goa, inaugurated in early 2024.

Focused Academic Environment

With a smaller student-to-faculty ratio than older NITs, it offers personalized mentorship and a tight-knit community feel.

Strategic Tech Growth

Fast emerging as a research hub in the Konkan region, with state-of-the-art labs for VLSI, Power Systems, and Data Science.

Goan Lifestyle

Located near the scenic coast, students enjoy a unique blend of high-level academics and a culturally rich, vibrant environment.

Many graduates have secured fully-funded PhD positions in prestigious institutions like NTU Singapore, Texas A&M, and various IITs.

Public Sector Leaders

Entrepreneurial Talent
