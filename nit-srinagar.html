Why NIT Srinagar?

Historical Legacy

One of the first eight Regional Engineering Colleges (RECs) established by the Government of India, carrying over 60 years of academic excellence.

Breathtaking Location

Situated on the western bank of the world-famous Dal Lake, providing one of the most serene and beautiful study environments in India.

National Integration

A melting pot of cultures with students from every state in India, fostering a unique environment of diversity and mutual respect.

Rising Placement

Significant jump in tech sector recruitment with major global firms visiting for on-campus drives in the 2025 session.
