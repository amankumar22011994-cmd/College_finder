About IIT BHU Varanasi

Historical Legacy

Founded by Mahamana Pt. Madan Mohan Malaviya in 1919 as Banaras Engineering College (BENCO). It became an IIT in 2012, merging century-old tradition with modern engineering excellence.

Vibrant Culture

Located within the massive 1,300-acre BHU campus, it offers a unique ecosystem where students witness both the spiritual essence of Kashi and high-tech research.

I-DAPT Hub

The institute leads the National Mission on Interdisciplinary Cyber-Physical Systems (NM-ICPS) through its I-DAPT Hub, focusing on smart city solutions and health-tech.

Civil Services Legacy

Famous for its "UPSC culture," IIT BHU has a massive track record of producing top IAS, IPS, and IFS officers, thanks to its intellectually stimulating environment.
