About IIT Madras

Legacy & Prestige

Founded in 1959 with West German assistance. India's #1 ranked institution (NIRF) for 8 consecutive years and home to a 617-acre forest campus inside Chennai city.

Research & Innovation

200+ research labs including NCCRD, NCMS, and ICSR. Files 150+ patents annually with a dedicated Research Park housing 250+ companies on campus.

Entrepreneurship

IITM Incubation Cell has funded 300+ startups. Notable ventures include Ather Energy, Satsure, and Mad Street Den. Hosts E-Summit annually.

Global Collaborations

Active MoUs with TU Munich, RWTH Aachen, NTU Singapore, and 80+ global universities. First IIT to set up an overseas campus (Zanzibar, Tanzania).

Campus Life
🦌

Forest Campus

617-acre campus is a protected forest with deer, monkeys, peacocks and blackbucks roaming freely — a unique urban jungle in Chennai.

🎭

Saarang

South India's largest cultural festival with 80,000+ footfall, celebrity performances, and competitions across arts, dance, music, and drama.

⚙️

Shaastra

One of India's premier science & tech fests. Robotics leagues, AI challenges, talks by Nobel laureates and global tech leaders.

🏊

Sports Facilities

Olympic-size swimming pool, cricket grounds, football fields, squash courts, badminton halls, tennis courts, and a well-equipped gym.

🏠

Hostels

16 hostels with guaranteed accommodation for all UG students. Each hostel has its own mess, common rooms, and cultural identity.

🍽️

Dining

Multiple mess halls, Himalaya Food Court, and canteens with South Indian, North Indian, and fast food options. Midnight canteens are a student favourite.

📚

Central Library

4 lakh+ books, e-journals, and digital resources. Air-conditioned reading halls open extended hours during exams.

🚀

Student Clubs

100+ clubs — Team Agnirath (solar car), Team Anveshak (AUV), astronomy, quizzing, robotics, music, dance, literature, and more.
