Why NIT Jalandhar?

Technological Core

Home to the ISRO S-TIC (Space Technology Incubation Centre), fostering research in space exploration and high-end tech projects.

Textile Tech Pioneer

One of the few premier institutes in India offering a world-class B.Tech in Textile Technology with specialized industrial ties.

Strategic Location

Situated on the G.T. Road bypass in Jalandhar, providing excellent connectivity to the sports and leather industrial hubs of Punjab.

Research Momentum

Ranked #202 in QS Asia (Southern Asia) 2025, emphasizing its growing influence in regional research and academic reputation.
