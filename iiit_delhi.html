About IIIT Delhi

Legacy & Prestige

Established in 2008 by the Delhi Government. One of the fastest-growing IIITs in India — recognized as a State University. Rapidly climbing NIRF rankings with a strong research mandate.

Research & Innovation

Strong research labs in AI, cybersecurity, bioinformatics, and HCI. 400+ research publications and multiple industry-funded projects. Faculty with top global university PhDs (MIT, CMU, Stanford).

Delhi Advantage

Located in Okhla, Delhi — easy access to the entire Delhi-NCR tech corridor including Noida and Gurugram. Best location advantage for internships, networking, and career growth.

Global Collaborations

Active exchanges and research partnerships with UMass Amherst, KTH Sweden, Nanyang Tech, and IIT Delhi. Students receive strong support for MS/PhD applications abroad.
