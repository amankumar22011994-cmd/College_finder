Why VNIT Nagpur?

Heart of India

Located in Nagpur, the geographical center of India, providing excellent connectivity and proximity to Ambazari Lake.

Architecture Hub

Ranked #10 in NIRF for Architecture, making it one of the premier destinations for design and planning in the country.

Industry Integration

Features specialized research labs in collaboration with top tech giants and a strong focus on Mining and Metallurgy.

Vibrant Culture

Famous for 'Aarohi', one of Central India's largest cultural fests, and 'Axis', its premier technical festival.

Campus Facilities & Culture
🏠

Green Hostels

Extensive hostel facilities for 3,500+ students, surrounded by nature and near the South Ambazari Road entrance.

🎭

Aarohi

The cultural soul of VNIT, a 3-day fest featuring pro-shows, literary arts, and musical competitions across Central India.

⚙️

Axis

A national-level tech fest showcasing robotics, innovative hackathons, and guest lectures from industry pioneers.

📚

Hi-Tech Library

Modern reading halls with massive digital journal subscriptions and a centralized computing center.

🏟️

Sports Complex

Includes a dedicated gymnasium, swimming pool, and vast fields for cricket, football, and hockey.

🕊️

Vihang

A flagship PG-focused sports and cultural fest, uniting M.Tech, M.Sc, and PhD scholars in unique team events.

In the Heart of Innovation

VNIT Nagpur is a center of excellence for both engineering and architecture in India.
