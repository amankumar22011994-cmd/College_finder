About NIT Surathkal

Coastal Heritage

Established in 1960 as Karnataka Regional Engineering College (KREC). It is the only NIT in India to have its own private beach, offering a unique campus experience.

Academic Excellence

Consistently ranked among the top 3 NITs. Known for its rigorous academic curriculum and high research output in Ocean Engineering and Information Technology.

STEP & Innovation

Home to the Science & Technology Entrepreneurs' Park (STEP), fostering a massive startup culture and providing incubation for student-led ventures.

Strategic Location

Located on the NH 66, perfectly nestled between the Arabian Sea and the Western Ghats, providing a scenic and technologically vibrant environment.

Campus Life
🏖️

NITK Beach

The crown jewel of the campus. A private beach where students relax, watch sunsets, and host events like surfing festivals.

🎭

Incident

One of the largest cultural festivals in South India. Famous for its 'Beach Pro-Show' and diverse competitive events.

⚙️

Engineer

The annual technical symposium. Features high-level hackathons, robotics competitions, and innovative project exhibitions.

🏢

Lighthouse

The Surathkal Lighthouse sits right next to the campus, offering a panoramic view of the coastline and the institute.

🚲

Green Campus

Strict anti-pollution norms. The campus is known for its lush greenery and eco-friendly transportation initiatives.

🍽️

Food & Mess

Variety of messes serving authentic Mangalurean, South Indian, and North Indian food. Night canteens are a student favorite.
