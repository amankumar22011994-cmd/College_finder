About IIT Guwahati

North East Pride

Established in 1994 as the 6th IIT. It is located on the banks of the Brahmaputra river and is often called the 'most beautiful campus in India'.

High Impact Research

Recognized globally for research in Bio-Sciences, Nano-Tech, and Energy. It consistently ranks in the Top 10 NIRF engineering category.

Strategic Location

The gateway to the North East, providing technical leadership in regional development, disaster management, and green energy solutions.

Global Outreach

Joint degree programs with Gifu University, Japan. Extensive collaborations with top universities in Europe, USA, and Australia.

Campus Life
🏞️

Scenic Beauty

Located on the banks of Brahmaputra with hills and lakes inside the campus. The view during sunset is world-class.

🎸

Alcheringa

The annual cultural fest of IIT Guwahati, one of the biggest in North India with diverse themes and pro-shows.

💻

Techniche

The annual techno-management fest hosting lectures, workshops, and the famous 'Guwahati Half Marathon'.

🏠

Hostels

11 Hostels named after rivers and mountains like Manas, Kapili, and Dihing. All have top-notch mess facilities.
