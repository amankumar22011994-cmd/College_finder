About IIT Jammu

Modern Infrastructure

Operates from its permanent campus at Jagti. The campus is designed as a sustainable smart city with state-of-the-art labs and high-speed connectivity in the lap of nature.

Strategic Research

Focuses on regional challenges like landslide mitigation, cold climate engineering, and sustainable energy. Collaborative research with defense and security agencies due to its location.

Collaborative Learning

Strong mentorship from IIT Delhi in its initial years. Currently has MoUs with various international universities and industry leaders for student exchange and internships.

Entrepreneurship (I2EDC)

The Institute Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre (I2EDC) supports student startups and provides a platform for converting ideas into scalable products.
