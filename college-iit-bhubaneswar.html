About IIT Bhubaneswar

Green Campus

Located at the foothills of the Barunei Hill, it is one of the most eco-friendly IITs with a fully functional permanent campus designed for sustainability and zero waste.

Ocean Research

Home to the "School of Earth, Ocean and Climate Sciences," focusing on niche research in monsoon prediction, marine biology, and climate change patterns.

Innovation Focus

Strong incubation cell for startups through the Design Innovation Centre (DIC). It actively encourages students to bridge the gap between academia and industry.

Global Outlook

Maintains active collaborations with global universities in USA, Canada, and Europe for student exchange programs and joint research initiatives.
