About IIT Kharagpur

Legacy & Prestige

Founded in 1951, IIT Kharagpur is India's first and largest IIT by campus size (2,100 acres). It is the mother institute of all IITs and holds a special historical significance in Indian technical education.

Research & Innovation

Home to 450+ research labs including centres for AI, Advanced Technology, and Rubber Technology. Files 300+ patents annually. Annual research funding exceeds ₹450 crore.

Entrepreneurship

Technology Entrepreneurship Park (TEP) and STEP incubation centre have supported 500+ startups. Notable alumni ventures include Tata Consultancy Services co-founders and Meritnation.

Global Collaborations

MoUs with MIT, Stanford, ETH Zurich, University of Tokyo, and 100+ global institutions. Hosts students from 30+ countries. Strong DAAD and Fulbright alumni exchange programs.

Campus Life
🏙️

Self-Sufficient Township

2,100-acre campus is virtually a self-sustaining township with schools, hospitals, post offices, banks, markets, and residential colonies — a city within a city.

🎭

Spring Fest

Eastern India's largest cultural festival with 60,000+ footfall. Hosts celebrity concerts, theatre, dance battles, literary events, and inter-college competitions.

⚙️

Kshitij

Asia's largest techno-management fest. Features robotics, AI hackathons, business case studies, talks by industry leaders, and innovation challenges.

🏊

Sports Facilities

400m athletic track, Olympic-size swimming pool, cricket grounds, football stadium, squash courts, badminton halls, tennis courts, shooting range and more.

🏠

Hostels

21 hostels including dedicated halls for women. All UG students are guaranteed on-campus accommodation. Each hall has its own canteen, recreation rooms, and yearly festival.

🍽️

Dining

Multiple hall messes, Nescafe outlets, Amul kiosks, and popular eateries like Madhusudan Das Hall canteen. Midnight canteens and food trucks are student favourites.

📚

Central Library

One of India's best academic libraries with 3 lakh+ books, 6,000+ e-journals, rare manuscripts, and 24x7 reading rooms during exams.

🚀

Student Clubs

150+ clubs — Aerial Robotics KGP, Team Robokon, astronomy club, quizzing society, KGPian Music Club, drama society, photography, and many more.
