Why IIT Hyderabad?

Fractal Geometry

Famous for its unique 'Fractal' design buildings and heat-resistant architecture developed with Japanese experts.

Japan Collaboration

Strategic JICA partnership. Huge opportunities for student exchange and direct recruitment into Japanese tech giants.

TiHAN Testbed

India's first real-world testing track for Autonomous Vehicles (Self-driving cars & drones) is right here on campus.

AI Leadership

IITH was the first institute in India to start a full B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence, leading the Industry 4.0 wave.
