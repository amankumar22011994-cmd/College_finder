Why NIT Rourkela?

Premier Ranking

Consistently ranked among the top 15 engineering institutes in India by NIRF, offering a reputation second only to top IITs.

Sprawling Campus

One of the largest residential campuses in India (645 acres), featuring its own airstrip, mountains, and modern facilities.

Diverse Research

Houses over 20 departments including unique ones like Ceramic Engineering and Food Process Engineering with high funding.

Global Alumni

A vast network of 40,000+ alumni leading major corporations worldwide and holding key positions in government and research.

BPCL Library

Nitrustave

The annual cultural fest, one of the largest in eastern India, attracting students from across the country.

⚙️

InnoVision

Central India's biggest technical fest, hosting robotics, coding marathons, and industry guest lectures.

🏔️

Mountain Border

Uniquely bordered by small mountains to the south, the campus offers a scenic landscape for recreation.

🏠

Halls of Residence

11 sprawling hostels providing a fully residential experience for over 7,000 students with 24/7 high-speed LAN.

🏟️

Sports Complex

Dedicated Dilip Tirkey Stadium and multiple courts for swimming, cricket, football, and indoor sports.

Distinguished Alumni (NITR Wall of Fame)

Sandeep Das

Former CEO, Reliance Jio

A visionary leader who played a crucial role in the initial rollout and strategy of India's largest telecom network, Jio.

CP Gurnani

Former CEO & MD, Tech Mahindra

One of the most respected names in the Indian IT industry, known for leading Tech Mahindra's massive turnaround and global expansion.

Dr. Soma Mondal

Former Chairperson, SAIL

The first woman to lead the Steel Authority of India Limited, she is a trailblazer for women in the Indian manufacturing and core sectors.

Nalin Shinghal

Former CMD, BHEL

An Electrical Engineering alumnus who led one of India's largest power plant equipment manufacturers at the highest executive level.
