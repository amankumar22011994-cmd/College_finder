About IIT Jodhpur

Future-Ready Focus

IIT Jodhpur is renowned for its focus on emerging technologies like Artificial Intelligence, MedTech, and Sustainability. It hosts a dedicated School of AI & Data Science.

Eco-Sustainability

The campus is a leading example of environmental consciousness, featuring massive solar power plants and water conservation systems designed for the arid Thar region.

Incubation & Growth

Through its 'JIDAN' initiative, the institute fosters entrepreneurship in robotics, clean energy, and healthcare, collaborating closely with AIIMS Jodhpur.

Global Linkages

The institute maintain strong academic ties with universities in Japan, France, and North America, offering students diverse international exchange opportunities.
