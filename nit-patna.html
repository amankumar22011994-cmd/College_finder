Why NIT Patna?

Historical Hub

The 6th oldest engineering college in India, offering a legacy that commands respect across the industrial sector.

Architecture Leader

Consistently ranked among the top 15 in India for Architecture and Planning (NIRF #13 in 2025).

Bihta Campus Expansion

Transitioning to a massive 125-acre state-of-the-art campus in Bihta to accommodate modern research and residential needs.

River-side Campus

The original Ashok Rajpath campus is uniquely situated on the banks of the River Ganges, providing a scenic environment.

Distinguished Alumni (NITP Legacy Wall)

Nitish Kumar

Chief Minister of Bihar

An Electrical Engineering graduate from the 1972 batch, he is one of the most prominent political figures in India and has served as the CM for multiple terms.

Bindeshwar Pathak

Founder, Sulabh International

A Padma Bhushan awardee whose revolutionary work in sanitation and social reform changed millions of lives across India.

Ratan Kumar Sinha

Former Chairman, Atomic Energy Commission

A renowned nuclear scientist who led India's nuclear program and served as the Director of Bhabha Atomic Research Centre (BARC).

Maneesh Kumar

Staff Software Engineer, Google

Representing the modern tech-focused alumni, holding key technical leadership roles in Silicon Valley's top software giants.

Bureaucratic Leaders

IAS, IPS & IES Officers

NIT Patna is legendary for its success in UPSC, with hundreds of alumni serving as top-tier bureaucrats and engineers in the Indian government.

Core Infrastructure Heads

Leaders in CPWD, Railways & PWD

As one of India's oldest colleges, its alumni have literally built the nation's bridges, roads, and railway networks over the decades.
