About IIT Kanpur

The KIAP Legacy

Established in 1959 with assistance from a consortium of 9 US universities (MIT, Stanford, Caltech etc.) under the Kanpur Indo-American Programme.

Academic Freedom

Pioneer of Computer Science education in India. Known for its rigorous academic curriculum and strong focus on fundamental research.

Infrastructure

Home to a National Wind Tunnel Facility and an autonomous airstrip for Aerospace research. One of the largest residential campuses in India.

Startup Ecosystem

Startup Incubation and Innovation Centre (SIIC) has supported 200+ companies. Leading in drone technology and cybersecurity research.

Campus Life
🏠

Halls of Residence

13 Halls. Known for "Hall Culture" and great autonomy given to students in management.

🎨

Antaragni

One of India's biggest cultural fests. Four days of music, dance, and literary events.

⚙️

Techkriti

Annual technical and entrepreneurial festival with participants from all over the world.

🛩️

Airstrip

IIT Kanpur is the only IIT with its own glider club and functional airstrip for research.

🌲

Peacock Campus

A lush green, sprawling 1000+ acre campus. Home to hundreds of peacocks and wildlife.

🍛

Canteens

MT (Main Temple) and various hall canteens that serve till 2-3 AM for late-night studiers.

🎾

Udghosh

The annual inter-collegiate sports festival with top-tier facilities for every major sport.

🚲

Cycle Culture

Vehicle-free academic zone. Almost every student and faculty uses cycles to commute.
