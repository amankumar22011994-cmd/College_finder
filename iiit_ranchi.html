About IIIT Ranchi

Legacy & Prestige

Established in 2016 under PPP model in Ranchi — Jharkhand's capital. Backed by Jharkhand government and industry partners. Focused on developing IT talent in eastern India.

Eastern India Gateway

Ranchi is the administrative and industrial capital of Jharkhand — a mineral-rich state with growing IT ambitions. IIIT Ranchi serves as a gateway for Eastern India's technical talent.

Research & Innovation

Active in data analytics, mining technology IT applications, and IoT research. Young faculty with enthusiasm for research. Student projects often tied to Jharkhand's mining and industrial sector.

Industry Connect

Growing connections with Ranchi's IT companies, SAIL, MECON, and heavy engineering firms. The Jharkhand government's IT push creates opportunities for government IT project participation.

Campus Life
🏠

Hostels

On-campus hostel facilities for all students with basic to modern amenities. Separate accommodation for boys and girls.

🎭

Aavishkar

Annual cultural fest with music, dance, and drama events bringing together Jharkhand's student community.

⚙️

CodeFest

Annual tech event with coding competitions and hackathons. Focus on applying technology to eastern India's unique challenges.

Ranchi Location

City of Waterfalls — Ranchi is Jharkhand's capital with pleasant climate. Dassam Falls, Hundru Falls nearby for recreation.

🍽️

Dining

Mess with North Indian meals. Canteen on campus. Ranchi's local food scene offers affordable and tasty dining options.

📚

Library

Campus library with engineering and IT resources. Digital access to academic databases for student research needs.
